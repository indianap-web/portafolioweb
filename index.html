More About Me

Let me introduce myself...

Hello!

My name is Indiana Peña, or as you've seen in my socials, Inddot. I'm a venezuelan graphic designer based in Tegucigalpa, Honduras. I've had the amazing opportunity to develop in this field for 1.5 years I absolutely love working with brand identity, visual branding and social media planning.

I believe design is more than sharing a message, it's sharing a feeling and an ideal. I love the little details in every art or design piece. I enjoy partaking in different and diverse projects and I'm always ready to take on new responsabilities and of course, meet new people, learn new things, gather experience and develop my skills (or new ones) in the process.

What I offer...

Have a glance at some of my skills!

Branding Identity

More than a logo, a brand is an unique representation of a solution or a feeling, I'll help you design a cohesive brand identity that can remain in your consumers' minds.

Creative Planning

Every creative project needs a meticulous plan, from phases to dates and budgetting, I can help you create a assertive plan for your brand and socials.

Social Media

How we portray our brands on the media is extremely important for our reputation, that's why I'll help you design visual content for your desired platforms.

Print Design

What are you dreaming today? From posters to flyers, banners, book covers, magazines and merchandising, I'm sure we'll create something great together after you check my portfolio.

Illustration and Custom Graphics

I really love drawing, specially if it's outside the digital, but I'm down to help you with vector illustrations, infographics and custom graphic projects.

Motion Graphics and Animation

I can create animated intros, explainer videos, or promotional video content. We can even add some motion to your logo for a dynamic digital presence!
